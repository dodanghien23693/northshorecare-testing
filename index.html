Our Cause

Helping People Manage their Incontinence and Live Full Lives Free from Stigma

Since 2002, NorthShore Care Supply has improved the lives of thousands of caregivers, parents, adults, and children by providing expert incontinence product recommendations and a wide range of incontinence products.

NorthShore specializes in high absorbency adult diapers, youth diapers, and other incontinence supplies. NorthShore can help if you are looking for products that are more absorbent than the retail incontinence brands so that you or your loved one can sleep through the night.

Discover life-changing products that contain leaks and odors discreetly while fitting your body properly. The right style and size of the product can give you added peace of mind and help maintain an active lifestyle.

Contact us today for our expert recommendations suited to your needs.
For more information on NorthShore please go to the About Us page.
